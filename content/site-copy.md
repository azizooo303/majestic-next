## NOTES FOR CANVA EDITOR / DEVELOPER

- All Arabic text is right-to-left (RTL). Ensure `dir="rtl"` and `lang="ar"` on all Arabic containers.
- Do not mix EN and AR in the same text node. Always use separate elements.
- Gold (#C1B167) used sparingly — CTA buttons and hover states only.
- Primary text color: #0c0c0c. Secondary: #484848.
- Headline font weight: 600–700. Body: 400.
- Arabic copy is written in Modern Standard Arabic (فصحى مبسّطة) suitable for Saudi B2B. It is not a translation — Arabic was written independently for the audience.
- All CTAs are invitations, not demands. Do not change CTA text to "Buy Now" or add urgency language.
